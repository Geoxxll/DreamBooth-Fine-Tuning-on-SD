# Training images

Place **3–5** photos of your **single subject** in `instance/`.

- Keep the subject clearly visible; vary angle/lighting slightly across images.
- Do **not** commit personal photos to public GitHub: `data/instance/*` is gitignored.
- For course submission, zip these images separately as required by the assignment.

The training scripts default `INSTANCE_DATA_DIR` to `data/instance` (see `configs/train_args.example.sh`).
